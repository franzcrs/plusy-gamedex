# Privacy Policy for PlusY Gamedex

**Last updated:** January 28, 2026

## Introduction

PlusY Gamedex ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our desktop application. Since PlusY Gamedex is a local desktop app that does not connect to the internet or collect personal data, our privacy practices are minimal.

## Data Collection

Our application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you or your device. We do not use analytics, tracking, or any form of data collection.

The only data handled by the app is information you voluntarily enter to manage your personal game collection, including:

- Game titles
- Remarks or notes
- Price information
- Custom fields you define

This data is stored exclusively on your local device in a JSON file within the app's data directory.

## Data Usage

The data you enter is used solely for the app's core functionality: searching, displaying, editing, and organizing your personal game collection. No data is processed or used for any other purpose.

## Data Retention

Your game data is retained on your device indefinitely until you choose to delete individual entries through the app's interface or remove the app entirely. We do not have access to or control over your local data.

## Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose any of your data to third parties. All data remains strictly on your local device and is never transmitted anywhere.

## Security

While we design the app to securely store your data locally, security ultimately depends on your device's protection measures. We recommend keeping your device secure with up-to-date software and strong passwords.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be included in updated versions of the app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
